The Place

Walking to class I saw this place right next door... didm't think much of it then.
Until, surprise! Our professor told us to make a film in 30 minutes!!
We chose this place in the Arts Center at NYUAD. It was in Level 1 beside the stairs...
the perfect place for our memory garden shoot.

The Shoot

The setting was serene, we decided to build the story from there. Youth tired from the
constant stress and build up of life- needing a chance to escape from
the sounds of the world...what better place than a lush green garden with seatings and
a small cozy library. Principle shots were taken by me, and Ziya took the role of acting!

The Props

I had my morning coffee with me, so we decided to use that asa prop. The shelves had a
collection of books, we chose one for the initial shots. We still had to bring in
a personal element, some intimate, something that would speak to the audience
then Ziya's quick thinking saved us!! She ran to her dorm and got her scrapbook, final touches
to the props!

The Film

The shooting was done...we edited the video in Capcut, ddded in some filters to give it
that vintage camera aesthetic. Then 5, 4, 3, 2, 1...SHOW TIME!

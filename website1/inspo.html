The Inspiration for this film was this poster I saw on Pinterest, of Ralph Lauren's Fall 2010 Home collection. The vintage camera aesthetic, matched with nature and vines, along with the old-style marble + bamboo combo inpired the style of The Memory Garden.

I wanted the colours to be muted, to go with the soft
vintage aesthetic, subtle and warm. The idea was to create
a scene in which viewers would feel at peace wiith their
mind, soul and body.
